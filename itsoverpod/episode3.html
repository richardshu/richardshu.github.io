Episode 3 - Greek Life & Boarding School - Henry Kim

July 15, 2020

Our friend Henry Kim talks about growing up in America as a foreigner, spending time in Texas for elementary and middle school, and moving to Pennsylvania for boarding school. We also discuss Greek life at NYU and UCLA and why so many college students choose to be a part of it.
