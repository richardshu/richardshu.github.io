About

What's up fam. Richard Shu and Patrick Ma go way back to elementary school. We met each other in 5th grade and did a lot of activities together including swim, tennis, debate, trombone, Team Fortress 2, and Monday book club. We even trick-or-treated together every year...

Anyways, we started this podcast as a fun side project to reconnect with our buddies and meet new friends. Every episode, we'll have a rotating guest with a unique story to share. Hope you'll join us for the ride.

Fun facts: Richard likes pineapple on pizza and Patrick does his own laundry.
