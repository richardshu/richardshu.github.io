Episode 1 - Rock Climbing, Boy Scouts, & Veterinary Desires - Brandon Tsai

July 1, 2020

Our first episode ever! We brought on our good friend Brandon Tsai to talk about his interest in rock climbing and how he founded a rock climbing club in high school. We also cover Brandon's experience as a Boy Scout including his path to Eagle and some camping highlights. And finally, we discuss what it's like to be on the pre-vet track as an undergraduate student majoring in biology at UC Riverside.
