Episode 4 - Social Activism & Thomas Jefferson - Amelie Lee

July 22, 2020

Growing up in a conservative, first-generation Asian American household can make it difficult and uncomfortable to talk about race relations in America. Amelie encourages us to challenge institutionalized and internalized racism, recognize that minority/ethnic groups face different obstacles, and keep an open mind by providing resources through her fast-growing Instagram account.

Follow @asianamericansforblm on Instagram and check out Amelie's podcast: Here's What I Think About That!
